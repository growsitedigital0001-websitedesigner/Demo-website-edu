Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Everything parents ask before joining BrightMind Academy — answered clearly.

What is the admission process and how long does it take? ▼
Our fully online 5-step process typically takes 24–48 hours. Fill the form above, submit documents via WhatsApp, attend a brief interaction, receive your offer letter, and pay fees to confirm your seat. Simple and fast.
Are scholarships available? Who is eligible? ▼
Yes! We offer merit-based scholarships from 25% to 100% fee waiver for students scoring above 85% in their previous board/school exams. Financial need-based scholarships are also available. Contact our team for details.
Is transport and hostel facility available? ▼
Yes, we have AC school buses covering 15 km radius across all major areas. Hostel facility with separate boys & girls wings, nutritious meals, study hall, and 24/7 security is also available for outstation students.
What medium of instruction is used? ▼
We follow English medium instruction for CBSE curriculum. For competitive coaching (JEE/NEET), we use bilingual instruction (English + Hindi/Marathi) to ensure complete concept clarity for all students.
Can I get a free demo / trial class before enrolling? ▼
Absolutely! Every prospective student gets a complimentary trial class in their chosen subject. This helps students and parents experience our teaching quality before making any commitment. Book via WhatsApp.
How can I track my child's progress? ▼
Parents get access to our digital Parent Portal where you can view attendance, test scores, homework submissions, teacher remarks, and upcoming events. Monthly PTM (Parent-Teacher Meetings) are also held regularly.
